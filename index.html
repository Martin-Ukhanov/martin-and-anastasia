Our Story

Martin & Anastasia were set up on a blind date by their two good friends at the beginning of 2022. They walked into it excited to meet someone new but not thinking much of it. They hit it off right away and from that day on they made a point to see each other as often as they could when time wasn't taken up by school or work. They often went on walks, cooked, listened to and made music, and talked about their future. The thing that remained at the centre of it all was their deep love and devotion for Christ which resulted in a deep love and devotion for each other.

On January 21st, 2023, Martin got down on one knee and asked Anastasia a very important question.
